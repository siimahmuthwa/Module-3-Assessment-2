Bacon Rings


PRICE: R29.90

Prep Time : 15mins

Yield : 6

Ingredients






Steps

  1. Heat a barbecue grill with hood on medium-high heat. Line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place onion rings on prepared tray. Place pineapple rings in centre of onion. Place string cheese, torn, over pineapple. Trim bacon rashers into straight strips and remove rind. Wrap around the onion and pineapple, overlapping, and threading through the centre.
  3. Spray with oil and sprinkle with paprika. Cook, with hood closed, for 10-15 minutes or until browned.
